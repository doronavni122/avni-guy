# Official sources standard (kit law)

## Sources section in published articles

- Heading: `מקורות` or `מקורות רשמיים` (Hebrew)
- Place at end of article body (before FAQ if present)
- Format: `מקורות: [Institution Name](url), [Institution Name 2](url2)`

## Anchor text rules

- **MUST** use the real institution or publication name as link text
- Examples: `משרד המשפטים`, `רשות המיסים`, `לשכת עורכי הדין`, `בנק ישראל`, `נט המשפט`
- **NEVER** use: `לשכה`, `מקור רשמי`, article slug, raw URL, or `slug | label` patterns
- **NEVER** use English slug as visible anchor text

## Authority

Prefer government (.gov.il), courts, bar association, and primary regulators over secondary blogs.
